# Director of Platform Engineering
## Chatham Financial

## Position Overview

The **Director of Platform Engineering** is a strategic leadership role responsible for leading the planning, development, and management of Chatham Financial's cloud infrastructure. This position oversees the design, implementation, and maintenance of cloud-based systems to ensure optimal performance, security, and scalability while driving innovation in cloud technologies and aligning cloud strategies with business objectives.

## Required Qualifications

### **Technical Expertise**

#### **Cloud Platforms**
- Extensive knowledge in **AWS Cloud** (primary platform)
- Deep understanding of cloud services including compute, storage, networking, databases, and security
- Experience with multi-cloud or hybrid cloud environments (preferred)

#### **Cloud Architecture**
- Strong knowledge of cloud architecture patterns and best practices
- Expertise in designing highly available, fault-tolerant systems
- Experience with microservices architectures and serverless computing
- Understanding of network architecture including VPCs, subnets, routing, and security groups

#### **Infrastructure as Code (IaC)**
- Expert proficiency in **Terraform** for infrastructure provisioning and management
- Experience with CloudFormation, AWS CDK, or similar IaC tools
- Understanding of GitOps principles and practices

#### **Container Orchestration**
- Expertise in container technologies (Docker, Kubernetes, Amazon ECS/EKS)
- Experience managing containerized workloads at scale
- Understanding of service mesh architectures (Istio, App Mesh)

#### **CI/CD & DevOps**
- Strong experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Proficiency with tools such as Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions, or AWS CodePipeline
- Understanding of DevOps culture and practices
- Experience with blue-green deployments, canary releases, and progressive delivery

#### **Observability**
- Expertise in monitoring, logging, and alerting solutions
- Experience with tools such as CloudWatch, Datadog, Splunk, Prometheus, Grafana, or similar
- Understanding of distributed tracing and application performance monitoring (APM)
- Ability to establish meaningful metrics and KPIs

#### **Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)**
- Strong understanding of site reliability principles
- Experience defining and tracking SLAs, SLIs, and SLOs

#### **Security & Compliance**

- Experience with identity and access management (IAM)

### **Industry Experience**
